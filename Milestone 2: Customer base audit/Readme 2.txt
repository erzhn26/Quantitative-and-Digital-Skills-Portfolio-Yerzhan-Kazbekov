Customer Decile Analysis
 Overview
This project analyzes customer data using decile segmentation to understand how different groups contribute to total revenue and profit. Customers were divided into ten equal groups based on their revenue contribution to identify high-value and low-value segments.
 Methods
- Decile segmentation  
- Percentile calculations and ranking  
- Revenue and profit distribution analysis  
 Tools
- Microsoft Excel  
- Statistical functions (e.g., `PERCENTILE.INC`, `AVERAGE`, `SUM`)
 Skills Demonstrated
- Quantitative data analysis  
- Spreadsheet modeling  
- Data interpretation and business insights
